Explain the risks

There is a small risk of rupturing the implants as a result of compressing the breasts for imaging, although there is currently no evidence of this. This risk is not a reason to avoid screening.

After the appointment, if you suspect an implant rupture or leak, consult your GP.

Difficulties taking images

Special techniques are used to minimise the risk of rupture and some additional images may be needed.

Difficulties interpreting images

Mammograms with breast implants can be less effective at detecting breast cancer because the implants can obscure some breast tissue.
